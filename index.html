About us

Established in May 2024, we began our journey with a humble pilot project for NEET UG, guiding just seven students toward their dreams. What started as a small initiative has since grown into a passionate endeavor to transform how students prepare for exams. With promising results from this batch-marked by their improved confidence and strong performance-we quickly gained momentum, proving the effectiveness of our approach.


Our mission

Many students lack clarity about the specific textbooks, study materials, or sources used to create exam questions. This knowledge gap frequently results in wasted time, misdirected effort, and undue stress during preparation. As a consequence, some students seek help in other cities, only to encounter substandard services. Dr. Khai, the founder and a successful exam candidate himself, is convinced that these challenges can be effectively addressed with the right approach and within HOMETOWN!
