Frequently asked questions

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) is a national-level medical entrance exam in India for students aspiring to pursue MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, and other medical courses in top medical colleges. It is the only medical entrance exam for admissions in government and private institutions.

To be eligible for NEET, a candidate must:

  • Be at least 17 years old at the time of admission.
  • Have completed or be appearing in Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) as mandatory subjects.
  • Have secured a minimum of 50% marks in PCB for General category (40% for SC/ST/OBC).
